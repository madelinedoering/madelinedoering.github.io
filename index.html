Madeline Doering

I'm a math-communicator, data enthusiast, and interdisciplinary learning designer working to put the femme in STEM and empower people to think critically about the world with learning, storytelling, and data. Currently I'm a Managing Editor at Complexly where I work on the educational YouTube channel Crash Course that reaches over 12 million subscribers. I've designed, launched, and facilitated digital and in-person learning experiences for K-12, college, and lifelong learners.

Previously I worked in data analytics and warehousing, dynamical systems, and the microfinance industry in Ecuador. When I’m not thinking about math or education, I’m reading, baking, crafting, looking for whales, or sampling new cheeses. I use she/her pronouns.
