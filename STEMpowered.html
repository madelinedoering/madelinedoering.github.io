Femme in STEM

Femme in STEM is a brainchild I developed with four partners while at the University of Denver. Using multiple grants from leadership and honors programs, we developed our own free program to empower girls ages 9-13 in STEM from across the Front Range of Colorado. Through three events and with over 150 participants, we crafted a one-day event that exposed girls to a breadth of specialized STEM topics, let them choose one to dive deeply into, and developed their confidence with our unique science fair system.
